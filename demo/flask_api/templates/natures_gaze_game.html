Nature's Gaze - Saccade Task

How to Play

  1. Focus on the image of the bird at the center of the screen.
  2. It will reappear either horizontally or vertically from the center of the screen.
  3. As soon as it reappears, you will be required to look either TOWARDS IT OR AWAY FROM IT as fast and as accurately as possible.

Then look back at the bird at the center when finished.
